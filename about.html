Who We Are

Jessore's #1 Bike Parts Store

Marjiya Auto Jashore (মারজিয়া অটো যশোর) has been serving motorcycle riders across Bangladesh since 2018. Located at RN Road, Jashore, we are the most trusted source for genuine motorcycle spare parts in the Jessore region.

We stock a comprehensive range of original and premium aftermarket parts for Bajaj, TVS, Honda, Yamaha, Hero, and other popular bike brands in Bangladesh. From fuel tanks and body kits to LED headlights and engine components — we have everything your motorcycle needs.

Our commitment is simple: provide genuine parts, honest prices, and expert advice so every rider can keep their motorcycle in peak condition. We offer home delivery across all 64 districts of Bangladesh, making premium bike parts accessible to everyone.
